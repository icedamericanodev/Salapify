## Full description

Where does your salary actually go?

Salapify is the money app that sides with you. Track your budget, debts,
savings, bills, and the people who owe you, all in one place, all stored
only on your phone. No account. No ads. No cloud watching your wallet.

BUILT FOR HOW WE ACTUALLY HANDLE MONEY

- Sweldo plan: every 15th and end of the month, a three tap plan appears.
  Log your income, move your savings first, check your budget. Done before
  the money can disappear.
- Utang list: track who owes you, and send a friendly reminder in English
  or Tagalog with one tap. Never feel shy asking for your money back.
- Bill reminders: your cards and loans warn you 3 days before the due
  date, with the exact minimum to pay to avoid late fees.
- Credit card statement forecast: see when your statement cuts, when
  payment is due, and what to pay to avoid interest.

MAKE IT A HABIT, NOT A CHORE

- Log an expense in two taps with quick add buttons.
- Keep your logging chain alive: 7 days of dots that never punish you for
  missing one.
- Weekly recap you can share: "7 of 7 days logged. Sana all."
- Celebrations when your net worth hits a new peak. Gold when you earn it.

KNOW YOUR NUMBERS

- Net worth, cash flow, and budget on one dashboard.
- Your own financial statements: balance sheet, income statement, and
  cash flow, like having a CFO for your life.
- Insights with spending by category, month by month.
- Savings targets on your accounts with progress bars.
- A notepad that does the math while you type: "grab 120 + 65" becomes 185.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

- Everything stays on your phone. Nothing is uploaded, ever.
- Optional fingerprint or face lock.
- Back up to a file you control: your Downloads, your own drive, your call.
- Free. No ads. No account. No tracking.

Also included: dark and light mode, two color themes (Forest and Mint),
home screen widgets for your budget and net worth, 20 currencies, CSV
export, and import from Peso Smart v1.

Salapify. On your money's side.
