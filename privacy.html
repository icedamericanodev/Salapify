Salapify Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

The short version: your financial data never leaves your phone. Salapify has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, and no ads. We cannot see your data even if we wanted to.

What data Salapify handles

Everything you enter in Salapify (accounts, balances, transactions, debts, people who owe you, goals, notes, and settings) is stored only in the app's private storage on your own device. It is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted anywhere by the app.

What we collect

Nothing. Salapify has no user accounts, no sign in, no analytics libraries, no crash reporters, and no advertising identifiers. We do not collect personal information of any kind.

Permissions the app asks for, and why

  • Notifications: only if you turn on reminders (payday, bill due dates, collecting money owed to you, or the daily logging nudge). All reminders are scheduled locally on your phone.
  • Biometrics (fingerprint or face): only if you turn on App lock. Authentication happens entirely through your phone's own system. Salapify never sees, stores, or has access to your biometric data.
  • Storage access via the system file picker: only when you choose to back up, export, or restore your data. Files are written where you point the picker, and only then.

Backups are in your hands

When you create a backup or CSV export, that file is yours: you choose where it goes (your Downloads folder, your own cloud drive, a chat to yourself). Treat backup files with care, since they contain your financial records in readable form.

Update delivery

The app checks Expo's update service (expo.dev) to download improvements to the app's own code. This is a standard, anonymous app update mechanism: your financial data is never part of these requests.

Children

Salapify is a general audience budgeting tool and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, children included, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if an optional cloud sync feature is added someday), the change will be described here and the app will ask before anything leaves your device. On device and private stays the default, always.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on the Salapify GitHub repository.
